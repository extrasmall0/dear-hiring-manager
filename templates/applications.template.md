# Applications tracker

One row per application. `status`: in-progress | skipped | filled | submitted | rejected | interview | offer
(`in-progress` = row created, outcome pending; `skipped` = gated by fit threshold or ineligibility;
`filled` = filled, parked before Submit). `flags`: count or short note of fields that needed human review.
