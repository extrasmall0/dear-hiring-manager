# Applications tracker

One row per application. `status`: in-progress | blocked | skipped | filled | submitted | rejected | interview | offer
(`in-progress` = row created, outcome pending; `blocked` = CAPTCHA/unreadable form, needs manual action;
`skipped` = gated by fit threshold or ineligibility; `filled` = filled, parked before Submit).
`flags`: count or short note of fields that needed human review.
